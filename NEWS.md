# mwmap 0.1.0

-   Initial CRAN release.
-   Functions for mapping Malawi at country, region, district, and traditional authority level: `mw_map()`, `mw_regions()`, `mw_ta_map()`.
-   Choropleth mapping via `mw_choropleth()`.
-   District highlighting with `mw_highlight()` and `mw_highlight_multi()`.
-   Point layer support via `mw_points()`.
-   Data joining utilities: `mw_join()`, `mw_clean_names()`, `mw_suggest_matches()`.
-   District name helpers: `mw_districts()`, `mw_districts_simple()`.
-   Label layers: `mw_labels()`, `mw_label_repel()`.
-   Consistent layout styling with `mw_layout()`.
-   25 Malawi-themed colour palettes via `scale_fill_mw()`, `get_mw_palette()`, `get_mw_palettes()`, and `view_mw_palettes()`.
-   Bundled spatial datasets: `mw_level_0`, `mw_level_1`, `mw_level_2`, `mw_level_3`, `major_lakes`.
